The Adventurer

The adventure is a journal writing game for a single player. During the game you will be creating a world and filling it with it's own people, traditions and folklore by journaling your characters adventures in it. All you need to play is something to log your journey in and a standard deck of playing cards.

Game play.

Game play revolves around drawing cards, interpreting it's meaning and then creating a journal entry to document your characters adventure. Cards a divided into four piles by suit, each suit meaning a different thing.

  • Spades - Locations
  • Hearts - Events
  • Diamonds - Things
  • Clubs - Beings

Drawing these cards you will tell the story of your adventure through an unknown land! Check out this video by { Lunch Break Heroes } that explains the game well.
