The Adventurer

{ Download from itch.io }



The Adventurer is a journal writing game for a single player played with a standard deck of playing cards. The game follows the life of a lone adventurer on a journey around a world of your own creation. During the game you will be learning about a world entirely of your own creation and the people and creatures that inhabit it.

Game play.

Game play revolves around drawing cards, looking up their meaning on the card table and then interpreting their meaning by writing journal entries.
